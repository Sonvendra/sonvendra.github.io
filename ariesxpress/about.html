Overview

ARIES XPRESS LOGISTICS PVT. LTD. is a leading cargo logistics and shipping services provider in India. With a strong commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses across various industries.

Our Journey

Since our inception, AXL has been dedicated to providing comprehensive logistics solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients. We have built a robust network spanning across India, ensuring timely and secure delivery of cargo through multiple transportation modes.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of logistics services including:

  • Air Cargo Services
  • Train Cargo Services
  • Surface Express Cargo
  • Warehousing Solutions
  • Packaging Solutions
