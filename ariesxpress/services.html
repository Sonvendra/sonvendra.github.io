Air Cargo

AXL provides fast and reliable air cargo services for time-sensitive shipments. Our air cargo services ensure that your consignments reach their destination quickly and safely. We have partnerships with major airlines and cargo carriers to provide seamless air freight solutions.

  • Express delivery for urgent shipments
  • Door-to-door service
  • Real-time tracking
  • Secure handling of valuable cargo
  • Nationwide coverage

Train Cargo

Our train cargo services offer an economical and efficient solution for bulk shipments. We leverage India's extensive railway network to transport your cargo across the country at competitive rates.

  • Cost-effective for bulk shipments
  • Wide network coverage
  • Safe and secure transportation
  • Regular scheduled services
  • Ideal for heavy and oversized cargo

Surface Express Cargo

Our surface express cargo service provides reliable road transportation solutions for all your logistics needs. With our extensive fleet and network, we ensure timely delivery of your shipments.

  • Door-to-door delivery
  • Part truck load (PTL) services
  • Full truck load (FTL) services
  • Express delivery options
  • Online tracking facility

Warehousing

AXL offers comprehensive warehousing solutions with strategically located facilities across India. Our warehouses are equipped with modern facilities and security systems to ensure safe storage of your inventory.

  • Strategic locations across India
  • Modern storage facilities
  • Inventory management
  • Secure and monitored warehouses
  • Value-added services

Packaging Solutions

We provide customized packaging solutions to ensure your products are protected during transit. Our packaging services include PVC packing, wooden crating, and other specialized packaging for fragile and high-value items.

  • Customized packaging solutions
  • PVC packing for quality handling
  • Wooden crating services
  • Protective packaging for fragile items
  • Expert packaging consultation
